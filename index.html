Who We Are

The Medilogic Group

The Medilogic Group LLC, is dedicated to specialty CNC engineering and manufacturing for the medical device community. From concept design to final product, the Medilogic Group is your single source for fast, accurate, high-quality medical devices and services.

Frank Occidentale

Frank has worked in a variety of manufacturing industries, including orthopedic manufacturer Stryker Corporation and cardiovascular manufacturer Boston Scientific. Wanting to utilize his varied experience and believing the need to merge prototype manufacturing with early research and development designs, Frank started the Medilogic Group. This allowed what has traditionally been a two stage process into the development of a single seamless, integrated process. This has resulted in Frank being able to provide excellent and specialized design, engineering and manufacturing services to the medical device community. As owner, Frank continues to grow and expand the Medilogic Group by successfully serving a multitude of clients.

Our People

The Medilogic Group employs the best talent in the medical and machining industries. All of our employees understand that through the application of their talents and capabilities they are helping to improve patients’ lives and well-being. It is because of their combined commitment as a team, the Medilogic Group is able to deliver our products and services on schedule with the highest quality and reliability.

Our Capabilities and Offered Services

  • In-House Design and Engineering

    We will work closely with you to take your concepts and ideas and develop them into working designs.

  • Prototype and Short-Run Production

    We are capable of handling all of your prototyping needs. We have proven results and can accommodate your needs whatever they may be.

  • Custom Designs

    Whether for a unique surgical instrument or a device resulting from an R&D project, we can fulfill your specialized design and needs.

  • Device Modifications and Reworks

    Have an existing product that needs modification for a new or different application? Let us help you modify or redesign the product to meet your new application. We have the experience and knowledge to help your company meet the required modifications of your instruments in a timely manner.

  • In-House Heat Treating

    The Medilogic Group has in-house heat treating capability for common surgical stainless steel, such as 17-4 PH and Custom 455.

  • State-of-the-Art Medical Materials

    We have extensive experience with various medical grade metals, including: titanium, stainless steel and medical grade aluminum. In addition, we work with all of the major medical grade polymers, including: PEEK and UHMWPE.

Qaulity and Lean Manufacturing

The Medilogic Group was established and continues to operate under the principles of delivering world class quality and service to our clients. We are dedicated and committed to customer satisfaction, continuous improvement, zero defects and on-time delivery. Our management team is committed to applying Lean Manufacturing and Modern Quality Management Principles as a management philosophy. It is through our lean manufacturing and quality management that we operate our entire system. It’s what ensures that we are always:

  • Focusing on the customer and providing value
  • Ensuring our employees have the tools and systems needed to be successful
  • Eliminating waste from our system
  • Ensuring that problems are visible and quickly addressed and resolved
  • Focusing on continuous improvement
